    No Visa Required (Economic and Diplomacy Alliance Act of 2020) | CANADA , CHILE, FRANCE, JAPAN, MALAYSIA, MEXICO, PHILIPPINES, POLAND, QATAR, RWANDA, SINGAPORE, SOUTH AFRICA, SOUTH KOREA, SPAIN, TURKEY, UAE, UK, USA

    Category Requirement
    Refugee (Valid for 3 Years) Fee: $0
  • Provide direct proof of persecution (Death threats, Police reports)
  • (If direct proof isn't applicable at this time) Provide indirect proof (News articles, Photos/Videos)
  • Have a valid Passport and Birth certificate
    		•
    Buisness/School Excursion (Valid for 3 Months) Fee: $80/Person
  • Provide a passport that is valid for a minimum of 6 months
  • Provide an invitation from a company/educational institution within Lingston
  • Provide address and phone number of your accommodation/hotel/hostel
  • Provide the total of invited employees/students
    		•
    Family (Valid for 1 Year) Fee: $0
  • Provide a passport that is valid for a minimum of 6 months
  • Provide PR or Citizenship certificates of the Family/Relative being visited
  • Provide address and phone number of your accommodation/hotel/hostel
    		•
    Work (Valid for 4 Years) Fee: $1,200
  • Have a valid passport
  • Must have been offered a job in Lingston
  • Must have a total of 13,000 Lingston Dollars or higher in Bank balance
  • Provide address of accommodation
    		•
    Student (Valid for 2 Years) Fee: $800
  • Have a valid passport
  • Be in good health
  • Have no criminal or immigration - related convictions
  • Convince an immigration officer that you have ties—such as a job, home, financial assets or family—that will take you back to your home country
  • Convince an immigration officer that you have financial support for your stay in Lingston or provide proof of financial stability
    		•
    Tourist (Valid for 4 Weeks) Fee: $2,200 or $0 If duration of stay is 1 week or less
  • Have a valid passport
  • Have a valid travel insurance
  • Proof and address of accommodation
  • Proof of a booked return flight ticket
  • Evidence of financial means
  • Must be 18+ unless accompanied by a legal guaridan
