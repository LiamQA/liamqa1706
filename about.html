Purpose

Our purpose is to create a harmonious society where diversity is celebrated, justice prevails, and every citizen contributes to the prosperity of our nation. The Imperial Republic of Lingston is dedicated to fostering community, preserving cultural heritage, and providing opportunities for growth and development.

Historical Highlights

The Imperial Republic of Lingston, founded in 2016, has a rich history woven with tales of resilience, unity, and progress. From the establishment of our Government to the modernization of our cities, each chapter in our history contributes to the vibrant tapestry of Lingston.

What is the IRL?

The Imperial Republic of Lingston, a micronation founded by HSC. Quentin in 2016 after the collapse of the 1st Republic, The Free Democratic Republic of Lingston, acknowledges its microscale nature without physical territory. However, we affirm our independence and sovereignty, eager to demonstrate our unique potential to the world.
